Our Services

At Supportive Solutions, we provide compassionate, individualized support for children, youth, and adults with autism and other developmental or physical disabilities. With over three years of hands-on experience in ABA therapy and support services, we are dedicated to helping individuals build meaningful skills and reach their full potential. Since 2020, we have worked closely with individuals as young as 2 years old through to adulthood, tailoring each approach to meet their unique needs. Our services focus on developing social skills, communication, and daily living abilities in a supportive and respectful environment. We understand that every family’s journey is different. That’s why we prioritize patience, consistency, and trust—creating a safe space where individuals feel comfortable, valued, and empowered to grow. In addition to therapy, we offer respite services to support families and reduce caregiver fatigue, providing reliable and compassionate care when it’s needed most. At Supportive Solutions, our goal is simple: to provide thoughtful, dependable, and high-quality support that makes a meaningful difference in everyday life.

We offer:
  • Respite care
  • One-on-one support
  • ABA services
  • Daily living support

Our Story

We are committed to supporting families across the GTA with compassionate, reliable, and personalized care. Our goal is to provide meaningful support that enhances quality of life and builds confidence for both individuals and families.
