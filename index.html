What We Do

We help children develop communication, social, and daily living skills using Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA). Our therapists work one-on-one with your child through structured play and real-life activities.

How ABA Therapy Works

1. Assessment

We meet your child and identify strengths, challenges, and goals.

2. Personalized Plan

A Board Certified Behaviour Analyst (BCBA) designs a custom program.

3. 1:1 Therapy

Your child works with a therapist using play-based and structured teaching.

4. Progress Tracking

We collect data every session and adjust goals regularly.

5. Parent Coaching

We teach you strategies so progress continues at home.
