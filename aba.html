What is ABA?

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) is a scientifically supported approach that focuses on understanding behavior and how it is influenced by the environment.

Our ABA Services

Our services are based on the principles of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA), an evidence-based approach recognized under the Ontario Autism Program (OAP).

Programs are individualized based on each person’s strengths, needs, and goals, and implemented using proven ABA strategies.
